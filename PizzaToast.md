

1. Put two slices of a bread on a tray.
2. Paint with thin layer of pizza sauce. 
3. Add Parmesan Romano and oregano.
4. Top with mozzarella or Mexican four cheese or both.
5. Add any spare fruits and vegetables and meats you have that look interesting in the context of food.
6. Sprinkle with crushed red pepper.
7. Oven at 425 for 10 minutes or until cheese has browned slightly.
8. Pair with grape lemonade.
